## About

You get an overview of the different aspects of literature mediation in the German-speaking countries, mainly the publishing system, journalism and literary distinctions. We focus on questions that treat the approach of society to literature and its consequences for literature mediation.

## Prerequisites and selection

### Selection

Selection is based upon the number of credits from previous university studies, maximum 165 credits.
