## About

The course treats how new religious movements (NRR) develop and change in a global perspective by partly problematise NRR from a theoretical perspective and partly through an advanced analysis of a number of representative global NRR. Central questions that the course treats are religious change, globalisation processes and creation of new religious expressions.

## Prerequisites and selection

### Entry requirements

Bachelor'sdegree or University studies equal to a Bachelor'sdegree, including an approved Bachelor thesis of at least 15 credits. Applicants must prove theirknowledge of English: English 5/English A from Swedish Upper Secondary School or the equivalentlevel of an internationally recognized test, for example TOEFL, IELTS.

### Selection

Selection is based upon the number of credits from previous university studies, maximum 165 credits.
