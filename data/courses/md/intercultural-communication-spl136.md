## About

The course develops your awareness and knowledge of cultural encounters, including an understanding of basic concepts and processes in the field of intercultural communication. Throughout the course, issues related to culture, language and communication are addressed with a particular focus on language use across cultural and linguistic boundaries to enable a sustainability perspective.

## Prerequisites and selection

### Entry requirements

General entrance requirements for university studies. Exemption is given from Swedish.

### Selection

Selection is based upon average grade from upper secondary school (34 %), the number of credits from previous university studies, maximum 165 credits (33 %) and Högskoleprovet - Swedish Scholastic Aptitude Test (33 %).
