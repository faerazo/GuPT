## Summary

In this course you get an insight into German-language literature and cultural theoretical issues and how these relate to a European context.

## About

NB: The course has scheduled online classes when given as a distance course.

## Prerequisites and selection

### Entry requirements

Filosofie Kandidatexamen, varav 60 hp tyska med minst 30 hp godkänt resultat från TY1210 eller motsvarande förkunskaper. Språkfärdighet i tyska motsvarande C1 (Europeisk referensram).

### Selection

Selection is based upon the number of credits from previous university studies, maximum 285 credits
