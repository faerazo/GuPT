## Summary

The theme of the course may vary. Examples of themes are discourse analysis, pragmatics, sociolinguistics, language history.

## About

You train your abiltity to describe and analyse linguistic concepts,

phenomena and theories that are included in the theme of the course.

You learn to apply linguistic theory and method in the analysis of course material and in your own production on the basis of the theme of the course. You also practice communicating in an idiomatically, grammatically and academically correct way in speech and in writing in English.

## Prerequisites and selection

### Entry requirements

To be admissible to the course, students must have completed the English linguistics 7.5 credits, in the Introductory course in English, 30 credits or the equivalent thereof.

### Selection

Selection is based upon the number of credits from previous university studies, maximum 165 credits.
