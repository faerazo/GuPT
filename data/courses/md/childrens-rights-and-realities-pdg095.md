## About

The course takes as its point of departure the 54 articles of the UN

Convention on the Rights of the Child. Important content areas are:

Theoretical constructs for describing and understanding children's

development and learning, childhood politics with specific focus on the

relationship between rights and realities in the everyday lives of

children and the implementation of the CRC in different countries, in

particular those the students come from.
