## About

The course Clinical Trials in Medicine provides an understanding of the regulatory and quality principles that govern clinical trials for drugs and medical devices. The course also provides an understanding of different types of study design, study protocol structure and content as well as the requirements for planning, conducting and completing a clinical trial. Required occasions occur in the course.

[course plan](https://kursplaner.gu.se/pdf/kurs/en/MED563)

[course](https://www.gu.se/sites/default/files/2021-11/MED563%20Literture%20list.pdf) [literature](https://www.gu.se/sites/default/files/2021-11/MED563%20Literture%20list.pdf)

## Prerequisites and selection

### Entry requirements

Admission to the course requires approved courses of at least 180 higher education credits. In addition, language skills equivalent to English A / English 6 are required.

### Selection

Selection is based upon the number of credits from previous university studies, maximum 285 credits
