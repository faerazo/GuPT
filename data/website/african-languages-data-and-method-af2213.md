## About

This course gives you the possibility to deepen your knowledge about

relevant research methods as well as data collection and processing.

Both general surveys on methods and data, and sample publications within each student's profile will be studied. During the course you will

analyse both structure and content of such research publications as well

as the research activities that they are based on. You will also design

your own research project for your planned master essay and conduct a

small pilot investigation resulting in a short PM. The course design as

well as the literature to be studied is chosen in a mutual dialogue

between you and your teacher.

**The course has scheduled online classes primarily daytime.**

## Prerequisites and selection

### Entry requirements

For eligibility to this course the completion of AF2208/AF2211 and AF2209/AF2212 is required, or the equivalent. In addition English 6/IELTS 6.5/TOEFL 580 is required in all parts, or the equivalent.

### Selection

Selection is based upon the number of credits from previous university studies, maximum 165 credits.
