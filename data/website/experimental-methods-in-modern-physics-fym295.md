## About

The purpose of the course is to give you as a student an insight into different types of experimental methods that are used in modern physics research, and to give you an opportunity to work with your own chosen well-defined experimental project. The projects are carried out in groups and are presented both in writing and as a presentation in a seminar.

## Prerequisites and selection

### Entry requirements

Bachelors degree in Physics or the equivalent. Applicants must prove their knowledge of English: English 6/English B from Swedish Upper Secondary School or the equivalent level of an internationally recognized test, for example TOEFL, IELTS.

### Selection

Selection is based upon the number of credits from previous university studies, maximum 165 credits.
