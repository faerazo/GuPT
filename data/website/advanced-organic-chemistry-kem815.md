## About

The course in Advanced Organic Chemistry provides in-depth and expanded knowledge as well as experimental skills in organic chemistry with a focus on synthetic aspects of complex organic compounds.

After this course, you should be able to

- carry out, plan and understand organic reactions at a deeper level
- perform qualified separation and structural analysis of organic compounds.

The knowledge and skills obtained in this course are necessary for further studies in organic chemistry and medicinal chemistry.

### Teaching

The course is divided into a theoretical part and a practical part where different types of learning activities are used.

This course is taught in English.

## Prerequisites and selection

### Entry requirements

50 credits within Chemistry, including KEM030, Organic chemistry or KEM031, Organic Chemistry for Students of Pharmacy, 15 credits or equal qualifications.

### Selection

Selection is based upon the number of credits from previous university studies, maximum 285 credits

## After graduation

The course can be included in the following programmes:

- Bachelor’s programme in Chemistry (N1KEM)
- Master's programme in Chemistry (N2KEM)
- Master's programme in Organic and Medicinal Chemistry (N2KEL)
- Master's programme in Chemistry and Learning (N2KOL).

The course can also be applied for as a stand-alone course.
