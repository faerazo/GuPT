## Summary

Learn to create and produce music from scratch with this course. No previous experience is required, only an interest and a desire to learn. Whether you are a curious beginner, an aspiring artist, or a passionate music creator, you get the chance to develop your musical and creative potential.

## About

Music production is more than just pressing record buttons – it is a journey from inspiration to finished and publishable music.

In this course, we will break down the process. Through tips and instructions concerning everything from music creation to mixing, you will learn how to create and produce music at a fundamental level.

When you have completed this course, you will be able to:

- **Independently plan and execute a musical production:** Discover how you organize your creative vision, set goals, and realize your musical ideas into a publishable song.
- **Master basic concepts:** Dive into the core principles of music production, including arrangement, sound design, and composition.
- **Analyse and describe music pieces:** Develop a critical ear and express your thoughts on different musical elements.
- **Create your own musical ideas:** Unleash your creativity by composing your own music.
- **Manage digital technology:** Learn the basics of digital audio workstations (DAW) and other necessary tools.

### Ready to Create?

Don’t miss this opportunity to explore your musical creativity with music production. Whether you dream of producing hit songs or simply want to express yourself musically, you can start here. Join us and let your creativity blossom!

### Teaching format:

- **Flexible learning:** Our course is entirely online and with a flexible schedule – learn at your own pace.
- **Recorded lectures:** Engage with expert-led video lectures that break down complex concepts.
- **Interactive sessions:** Connect with fellow students and teachers through interactive discussions and collaborative assignments.

The language of instruction is English but of course, your music can be written and produced in the language you prefer.

### Prerequisites

- **Computer and software:** You need access to a computer (or tablet) with sufficient performance for online studies and basic music production. Furthermore, some form of DAW software (Digital Audio Workstation) such as Logic, Ableton Live, Cubase, Studio One, Pro Tools, GarageBand, Reaper, Bitwig, FL Studio etc.
- **Prior musical knowledge:** Even if not necessary, it’s a clear advantage if you have some experience of creating or performing music.
- **Sound:** Consider investing in good headphones or speakers, as well as an external microphone and sound card to create optimal audio conditions.
