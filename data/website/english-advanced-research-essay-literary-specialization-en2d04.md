## About

This course aims at giving students an opportunity to work independently under supervision on an in-depth study of issues relating to both theory and practice in English literary studies. By applying such theories and also relying on relevant research, students are expected to write a scientific research paper on a selected topic in the field resulting in a 15 hec Master thesis in English-language literature.

## Prerequisites and selection

### Entry requirements

A pass grade in EN2D12 or equivalent.

Valid from spring2022:The requirement for admission to the course is a Bachelor's degree including an individual assignment of at least 15 credits, or the equivalent in a foreign degree, and at least 15 credits in literary courses in English as the main field of study at second cycle level.

### Selection

Selection is based upon the number of credits from previous university studies, maximum 165 credits.
