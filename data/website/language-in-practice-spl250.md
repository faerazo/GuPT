## Summary

The purpose of the placement is to give you the opportunity to apply your language skills in a work situation, and to identify potential future career paths in Sweden or abroad.

## About

This course includes a half-semester long placement at a workplace where you perform tasks related to language. You are responsible yourself for finding a suitable placement, which could be a translation agency, embassy, cultural institution, etc. In addition to the placement, the course includes seminars, as well as the submission of a final placement report.

You find more information about the content and structure of the course in the student portal: [https://studentportalen.gu.se/english/my-studies/sprak/internship](https://studentportalen.gu.se/english/my-studies/sprak/internship)

## Prerequisites and selection

### Entry requirements

To be admitted to the course, students must fulfill general entry requirements for higher education at first cycle level, including English 6.

A further requirement is completed courses (optional) from second cycle of at least 15 credits, or the equivalent.

### Selection

Selection is based upon the number of credits from previous university studies, maximum 165 credits.
