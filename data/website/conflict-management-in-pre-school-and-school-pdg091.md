## About

The course aims at enlarging awareness, insight and knowledge in

conflicts and conflict management through the introduction of prevailing

theories and practices. Conflicts that emerge in the context of the

preschools and schools are in focus. The course provides the students

possibilities for reflections and introspection of own experienced

conflicts in these contexts. Furthermore, students will be given the

opportunity to transfer knowledge in conflicts and conflict management

to the profession of the teacher.
