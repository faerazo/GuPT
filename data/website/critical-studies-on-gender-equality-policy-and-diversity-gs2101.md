## About

In this course you will acquire theoretical and methodological tools to critically scrutinize and develop gender equality and diversity management. Particular focus is on exclusionary norms in politics, working life and voluntary organizations. We will work with intersectional perspectives and make intersectional analyzes of norms that exclude and include.

## Prerequisites and selection

### Entry requirements

Bachelor's degree or equivalent.

### Selection

Selection is based upon the number of credits from previous university studies, maximum 165 credits.
