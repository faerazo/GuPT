## About

In the course, scripts for film, TV, and video games are analyzed from the perspectives of traditional film style-oriented film studies, adaptation studies, literary text analysis, and drama research, as well as game research and studies of script processes.

## Prerequisites and selection

### Selection

Selection is based upon the number of credits from previous university studies, maximum 165 credits.
